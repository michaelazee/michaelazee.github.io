About


Hello! My name is Michaela Zee and welcome to my site!

Michaela Kitlin Zee was born on November 21, 2000. She originally pursued acting and voiced several Disney characters. This included Ginger on Phineas and Ferb, Princess Jun on Sofia the First, and Nafisa in Wreck-It Ralph 2. She is currently studying at The New School as a Journalism & Design major with a minor in Fashion Studies. This website contains her work about fashion and her life in New York City.
