About


Michaela Kitlin Zee was born on November 21, 2000, in Santa Monica, California. She grew up in Calabasas with her mother and father, Ming-Na and Eric Zee. She also has a little brother named Cooper and a Havanese named James. Michaela originally pursued acting and voiced several Disney characters as a teenager. This included Ginger on Phineas and Ferb, Princess Jun on Sofia the First, and Nafisa in Wreck-It Ralph 2.

Michaela is currently studying at The New School as a Journalism & Design major with a minor in Fashion Studies. She also interned for some companies within the fashion industry, including Young & Reckless and Brooklyn PR. This website contains her personal work about fashion and her life in New York City.
